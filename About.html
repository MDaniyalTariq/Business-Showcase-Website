About Hilton Enterprises

Who We Are

Hilton Enterprises has been a pioneer in the Apparel and Manufacturing industry in Pakistan. The enterprise today caters to various segments within the garment industry. Our Strategic Business Units specialize in the manufacturing and export of Leather Garments, PU Garments, Leather Gloves, Motorbike Suits and accessories, Textile Garments, and Sportswear.

Hilton Enterprises was established in 1987 by Tariq Mehmood in Sialkot, Pakistan. The enterprise currently boasts an annual turnover of over USD 30 million. Hilton Enterprises is a trusted and esteemed name in the business realm, renowned for its financial stability and impeccable creditworthiness. The enterprise proudly employs a talented workforce of over 1500 individuals across all its units and departments.

As a frontrunner in the industry, sustainability remains central to our business ethos. We never stagnate. Continuously, we dedicate ourselves to fostering sustainable relationships with our customers, environment, and community.
